      About Page

      • About Page:
        Weekly Teacher Tasks is an app that helps teachers manage their daily to-do list. The main goal is to have teachers organize theirs task in to various categories and to help manage their time more effectively. I believe time and learning to manage time is essential to all educators across the globe.
